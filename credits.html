Meet the team!

Tyler Nykilchyk

Tyler Nykilchyk is a queer, neurodivergent, interdisciplinary artist, working with photographic processes, filmmaking, drawing, textiles, and sculpture. Currently working as the Production Coordinator for Debaser and their "Pique" Festival. He's a graduate of the diploma program at SPAO (School of the Photographic Arts: Ottawa) and is currently studying Interactive Multimedia and Design at Carleton University.

He has exhibited at the Ottawa Art Gallery, CONTACT Photography Festival, and Roseberry Road studios (Bath, UK). He has screened his short films in festivals in the UK, Egypt, Vancouver, Winnipeg, Toronto, Gatineau, and Ottawa.

Josias Ngendahimana

My name is Josias Ngendahimana. I grew up in Kigali, Rwanda, where I founded my first company in the creative industry called CREADE. The company is currently operating back home in Rwanda, and my vision is to expand it to Canada after I graduate. I'm currently in my first year of the Interactive Multimedia Design program, and I'm passionate about using creativity and technology to bring ideas to life across borders.


Jonas Voorn

Jonas Voorn is a 20 year old design student, with an interest and focus in fashion design and art. Coming from rural Ontario, Jonas seeks greater opportunities in the capital city of Ottawa, while attending Carleton University under the Interactive Multimedia and Design program.

Jonas currently runs a clothing brand named Spliced United, selling custom T-shirts and sweaters online.
